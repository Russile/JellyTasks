# Privacy Policy for Jelly Tasks

Last Updated: December 2024

## Introduction
This privacy policy explains how Jelly Tasks ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our browser extension.

## Information We Collect
The extension collects and processes the following data:
- Google account email (for authentication purposes)
- Google Tasks data (your tasks, task lists, and task details)
- Extension preferences and settings

## How We Use Your Information
Your information is used solely for:
- Authenticating you with Google Tasks
- Displaying and managing your Google Tasks
- Storing your extension preferences locally
- No data is collected for marketing or analytics purposes

## Data Storage and Security
- Task data is accessed directly from Google Tasks API
- Extension preferences are stored locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API
- We do not maintain any separate databases or servers
- All communication with Google's servers is encrypted using HTTPS

## Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- Google Tasks API: For task management functionality
- Google OAuth 2.0: For secure authentication
Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle your data.

## Data Retention and Deletion
- Extension data stored locally can be removed by uninstalling the extension
- Task data remains in your Google account and can be managed through your Google account settings
- No data is retained by us after extension removal

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your Google Tasks data through your Google account
- Delete your local extension data by uninstalling the extension
- Revoke the extension's access through your Google account settings

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact
For any questions about this privacy policy, please visit https://github.com/Russile/JellyTasks/blob/master/privacy_policy.md.
