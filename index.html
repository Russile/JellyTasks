Jelly Tasks

Quick access to Google Tasks from your browser

About Jelly Tasks

Jelly Tasks is a browser extension that provides seamless integration with Google Tasks, allowing you to manage your tasks directly from your browser's toolbar. With Jelly Tasks, you can create, edit, and organize your tasks without leaving your current webpage.

Features

  • Create and manage tasks directly from your browser
  • Organize tasks into different lists
  • Set due dates for your tasks
  • Mark tasks as complete/incomplete
  • View all your Google Tasks lists in one place
  • Seamless integration with Google Tasks

How It Works

Jelly Tasks connects securely to your Google Tasks account, allowing you to access and manage your tasks without leaving your browser. The extension uses official Google Tasks API for all operations, ensuring your data remains secure and synced across all your devices.

Data Privacy

We take your privacy seriously. Jelly Tasks only accesses your Google Tasks data with your permission and does not store any data outside of Google's services. All operations are performed through the official Google Tasks API.
